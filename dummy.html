ABOUT US

DIRE, which means presaging disaster, is an interactive web-based platform designed to empower tourists visiting Bali with the knowledge needed to respond effectively to earthquake and tsunami disasters. True to its name, the web app equips users to confidently navigate DIRE situations, turning potential disasters into well-managed responses. Through engaging and interactive scenarios, DIRE educates users on the risks associated with these natural disasters, while providing crucial safety measures that can increase the chances of survival during an event. Our goal is to bridge the gap between disaster preparedness education and travelers, offering a user-friendly and informative experience that ensures visitors understand the importance of being prepared when traveling to areas vulnerable to natural disasters. Our slogan, *Disaster? Ready!* reflects our commitment to equipping tourists with the confidence and knowledge needed to stay safe in the event of an earthquake or tsunami, turning uncertainty into preparedness.

To ensure that the content on DIRE is accurate and up-to-date, we have partnered with BPBD Bali (Bali Regional Disaster Management Agency) to source official materials and insights on earthquake and tsunami mitigation strategies. BPBD Bali's expertise in disaster risk management has been invaluable in creating realistic, region-specific scenarios that reflect the unique hazards and emergency protocols in Bali. Through this collaboration, we aim to provide tourists with authentic, actionable information that aligns with local disaster preparedness standards. BPBD Bali's contribution not only strengthens the educational impact of DIRE but also helps reinforce the importance of community resilience and effective disaster management in protecting both locals and visitors alike.
